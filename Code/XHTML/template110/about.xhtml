About Us

When we opened our first store in 1950, our aim was to offer the very best in widget retailing with a wide range of competitively priced products backed up with expert advice and the highest standards of customer service.

Over sixty years later, our commitment is unchanged even though our business now spans an entire continent and has expanded to cover sales via the internet as well as in our local stores.

Our commitment to customer service remains fundamental to all our activities. We invest heavily in training our sales staff and so customers can be confident that the product we recommend is the one to meet their needs.

In a changing world, our commitment to these core activities ensures that our company will maintain its position as the leading supplier of widgets to your industry.
